Government of Kenya Monitoring, Evaluation, Reporting, and Learning

This Monitoring, Evaluation, Reporting and Learning (MERL) tool is a structured digital system designed to track the progress, effectiveness, and impact of projects, programs, or organizational strategies across counties. It provides a systematic approach to collecting, managing, analyzing, and reporting data, enabling evidence-based decision-making and accountability.
