About MERL Tool

The Monitoring, Evaluation, Reporting and Learning (MERL) Tool is a digital platform developed to support the Ministry of Environment, Climate Change and Forestry (MECCF) in Kenya to monitor, track and assess progress across its key thematic areas:

  • Climate Change Adaptation and Mitigation
  • Forestry, Agroforestry & Landscape Restoration
  • Biodiversity & Ecosystem Conservation
  • Waste Management & Pollution Control (Circular Economy)
  • Cross-Cutting Enablers (Governance, Policy, Finance, MERL, Capacity)

The platform provides a centralized system through which environmental and climate-related data can be collected, managed and analysed at both national and county levels.
