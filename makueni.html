Smallholder Climate-Smart Irrigation Scheme

Drip irrigation and on-farm water harvesting for smallholder farmers to reduce climate risk, stabilize yields, and support high-value crops.

FY 2024/25 Makueni County Agriculture

Ward Access Roads & Flood Control Structures

Upgrading critical access roads with drainage and flood-protection structures to keep markets and health facilities reachable during heavy rains.
